Welcome to Whiskey'd Away - Your Passport to Whiskey Adventures in the UK

At Whiskey'd Away, we invite you to embark on an extraordinary journey through the captivating world of whiskey, right here in the heart of the United Kingdom. As passionate aficionados of the 'water of life,' we have curated a series of unforgettable whiskey tours that will take you on a captivating exploration of Scotland's and the UK's rich whiskey heritage.

Discover the Essence of Whiskey

Our mission is simple but profound: to offer you an authentic, immersive, and educational whiskey experience. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or a curious newcomer to the whiskey scene, our tours are designed to suit every palate.

Why Choose Whiskey'd Away?

  1. Expert Guides: Our knowledgeable guides are not just experts in whiskey but also storytellers who will breathe life into the history and culture surrounding each distillery you visit.
  2. Handpicked Distilleries: We've scoured the UK to bring you the finest distilleries, each with its own unique charm and character. From the picturesque landscapes of Scotland's Highlands to the historic distilleries in the heart of London, we offer a diverse range of experiences.
  3. Tasting Experiences: Savour a wide array of whiskey styles and flavours, from smoky Islay malts to the smooth, honeyed notes of a Speyside dram. Our tastings will tantalize your senses and deepen your appreciation for this noble spirit.
  4. Exclusive Access: Enjoy behind-the-scenes access to distilleries, meet master distillers, and witness the craftsmanship that goes into every drop of whiskey.
